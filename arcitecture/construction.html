Construction

Our pioneering, integrated approach means we take control of your project from concept, through construction and fit-outs, to final handover. We are responsible for every element of the design and build, including the construction phase. Confident in this method, we guarantee that your project will be delivered as promised within the time agreed.

Getting involved from the outset

Our experienced in-house construction team gets involved at the very outset of the project. They work with our architects to ensure the design vision can be translated into a reality. That means you can be sure the project will be built as originally planned, reducing expensive changes or delays.

A hassle-free construction phase

Our Design-Build-Operate-Transfer (DBOT) approach means you can be confident of a hassle-free construction phase. We project manage every element of the build, from following planning regulations to overseeing security and safe handling of materials.

An experienced, skilled team

Our in-house construction team covers every skill for the successful delivery of your project. This includes experienced supervisors, masons, carpenters, and painters. We believe craftsmanship is essential to deliver a unique look and feel to a space, and we make the most of the talents of our team.

Keeping you in control

We want you to feel in control of the construction of your home or commercial building. Our detailed construction plans and Gantt. charts give you a clear timeline, so you can stay on track with what will happen and when. We’ll define your role as well so that nothing can delay the project.
